Establishing Interplanetary Existence

The year is 2077 and mankind has managed to colonize other planets in the solar system. The Earth is no longer the only planet to support human existence.
In such an vast network of planets space travel becomes an essential need.

SPACE TRAVEL MADE ORGANISED AND SAFER

Dusk is a universal space traffic control service that detects any object/spacecraft in the vicinity of another spacecraft and guides it to take appropriate action (stop/wait/go) through a traffic light installed at regular intervals on all authorized space travel routes.

WORKING

We have installed cameras, processing units and motion trackers on the traffic signals which capture videos that are broken down into frames.
This will be used to calculate the traffic density on that particular route and then accordingly manipulate the traffic signal on the basis of the instructions generated through the AI model.

THE PROBLEM

With the colonisation of all planets in the solar system and commercialisation of space travel, lakhs of spacecrafts are travelling at the same time in space. Moreover, 27,000+ pieces of orbital debris, or “space junk,” has been tracked. Much more debris, too small to be tracked, but large enough to threaten human spaceflight and robotic missions, exists in the near Earth space environment.

To regulate their movement, and to avoid collisions between orbital debris/meteoroids or asteroids, Dusk has come up with a simple yet effective solution.

OUR SOLUTION

The concept of a traffic light on roads has been utilised with various alterations making it suitable for use in space. We have made a self-sufficient traffic system infused with AI, Deep ML which will be integrated with a processing unit and motion tracker fitted on each traffic light with an external connection to a camera.

The traffic lights would alter their duration on the basis of a pre-fed algorithm in the processing unit module which in turn will optimize the availability of routes and reduce congestion. The traffic light will be solar powered and thus will be in working condition 24/7. We would also be adding batteries for a backup.

THE UNIQUE COLOURS

Blue instead of green - Blue is one of the rarest colours in the universe

Orange instead of yellow - Yellow could not be used as many stars emit yellow light.

Pink instead of red - Red light is emitted by a lot of the suns in space
